About Kenyan Underground Rap

We are a platform dedicated to amplifying the raw talent of Kenya’s underground rap scene. From Nairobi’s streets to Eastlands cyphers, we celebrate emerging artists, drill waves, and alt hip-hop pioneers.

Our mission is to provide a space for undiscovered voices, foster a community of artists and fans, and push the boundaries of Kenyan hip hop culture.

Join us as we amplify the underground—because every voice deserves to be heard.
